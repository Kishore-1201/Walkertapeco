Ready To Work For Walker Tape®?


We're Looking for Amazing People.


We strive to make working at Walker Tape® an awesome experience. Our team is full of exceptional individuals who we truly value, and we love being a part of their lives.

As a result, we are looking for awesome people to join our team. It is important for us to hire people who have an own-it attitude, are team players, and are constantly learning and striving to improve.

If this sounds like you, keep reading to learn more!

HOW YOU’LL FIT IN


Treating people well is the number one thing we can do to remain successful. That goes for our customers, suppliers, and employees.

We reward our employees for their hard work during their careers at Walker Tape®. As a member of our industry-leading company, you'll have the opportunity to grow and progress.

Our employees are promoted from within, and we love watching them grow and thrive in their personal and professional lives.


Read More


Here Are Just a Few of the Perks You’ll Have as a Walker Tape® Employee:

 

TAKE PTO FROM DAY ONE


Once you're hired, you'll be granted 44 hours of paid time off. After six months, you'll earn an additional 60 hours.

You'll continue to gain PTO based on your years with Walker Tape®. Once you've reached your fifth year, you'll have earned 184 total allotted hours.

We want you to be able to get out and do fun stuff!

KEEP LEARNING


Going along with our Core Values, we want to help you grow and flourish!

Seeing our employees improve their craft is what we live for and helps us stay ahead of the game.

We'll send you to conferences and buy you books to help you learn something new. Mentors, classes, and trainings are how we grow as people and pros.

BENEFITS


You'll be a W-2 employee with fantastic health insurance. Walker Tape® covers 90% of the premiums for your medical, vision, and dental insurance.

We cover 100% of the premium for a $50,000 life insurance policy for you, and $10,000 for your spouse and each of your dependents!

We also have bonus opportunities. When Walker Tape® wins, so do you.


A Bit About Us


We’re the industry leader in tape and adhesive for hair replacement systems and are expanding into other industries. So, what does that mean? The easiest way to break it down is that we make tape for hair replacements, hair extensions, and commercial applications.

We’re profitable, resourceful, and growing. Walker Tape® was started in 1985 and we haven’t taken a break yet. We’ve loved building this business which has boosted people’s self esteem and has made a positive impact on people’s lives. While we’ve grown a lot in the last few years, we’re working hard to maintain a close-knit team culture where everyone is excited to come to work and passionate about the work they do.

Walker Tape® is an equal opportunity employer. We believe that diversity is an asset and that backgrounds, beliefs, and different experiences are critical to our success. Walker Tape® is a welcoming, supportive, and collaborative environment for all of our employees. Our main focus is to create a diverse, hard-working, and sharp group of team players who have fun and work together.
